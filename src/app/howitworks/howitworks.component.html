How it Works?

This application is in BETA currently. You can try out the application on the Ropsten Testnet. Please follow the suggested steps.

1

The borrower applies for a loan against their commodity.

2

The lender can select the preferred commodity to lend against.

3

Lender needs to click on the "LEND" button to check the transaction details. You would be shown the details of the transaction. Visit https://faucet.ropsten.be/ for receiving test ETH on the Ropsten Network.

4

Sign your transaction with MetaMask, which is essentially a gateway from your browser to blockchain apps. You can install it from https://metamask.io/
